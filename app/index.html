About me

Hello, my name is James and I'm a full-stack web developer specialising in frontend Javascript development based in Bristol. I develop anything digital, including websites, mobile apps on Android and iOS and make games on a variety of platforms. You can take a look at some of my personal projects here, and read my webish ramblings here.

Follow me on Twitter, or you can get in touch with me via email.

In my day job I work with large brand clients, including RAC, Sky, Honda, Sainsbury's, PHS and BP. I work at Rockpool Digital as a frontend developer, using technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, Grunt, Sass and Javascript.

This site is built in Django and hosted on Webfaction; the frontend uses Grunt, Sass, and Backbone.
